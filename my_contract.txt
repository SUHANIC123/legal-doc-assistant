THIS MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of this 1st day of May, 2024 (the “Effective Date”),
by and between Global Tech Solutions Inc., a Delaware corporation, having its principal place of business at 1000 Innovation Drive,
Palo Alto, California 94301 (“Client”), and Data Dynamics LLC, a New York limited liability company, having its principal place of
business at 200 Madison Avenue, New York, New York 10016 (“Provider”).

1.  SERVICES. Provider shall provide Client with data analytics and cloud infrastructure management services (the “Services”)
as more fully described in Exhibit A attached hereto. Provider shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike
manner and in accordance with industry standards. Provider acknowledges that the timely performance of the Services is
of the essence to this Agreement.

2.  TERM AND TERMINATION. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for an initial term of
three (3) years (the “Initial Term”). Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for
successive one (1) year periods, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal to the other party at least
ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause
(i) upon a material breach by the other party if such breach is not cured within thirty (30) days of written notice,
or (ii) if the other party becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy.

3.  FEES AND PAYMENT. In consideration for the Services, Client shall pay Provider a fee of $250,000 per year, payable in
equal monthly installments of $20,833.33. All invoices are due within thirty (30) days of receipt. Late payments shall be
subject to a finance charge of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance.

4.  CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party (as a “Receiving Party”) agrees that it shall not, during the term of this Agreement or
thereafter, disclose or otherwise make available to any third party any Confidential Information of the other party
(as a “Disclosing Party”). “Confidential Information” shall include any non-public information, including technical data,
trade secrets, business plans, and customer lists.

5.  LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY’S AGGREGATE LIABILITY ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT
EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID BY CLIENT TO PROVIDER IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM. IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER
PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.
